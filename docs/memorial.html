Mollie Dent Memorial Service

Sunday May 5th, 2024

Please join us to honor Mollie Dent with a memorial service and celebration of life on Sunday May 5th at the Lakeview Picnic Area, Vasona Park in Los Gatos from 10am to 4pm.

You are welcome to come any time during the day. The picnic area is booked starting at 10am with bagels and spread available for a light brunch. Barbeque and lunch will be served starting at 1pm.

RSVP's are not necessary but appreciated, please share this invitation with anyone who might wish to join. If you'd like to assist with providing food or drinks, contact James Pettit via email or text.
